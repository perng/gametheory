Rules

  • Black starts first.
  • Moving side kills opponent's piece when:
    1) moving side moves 2 pieces together,
    2) one piece is next to opponent's piece,
    3) only 3 pieces are on this row/column.
  • One side loses when has < 2 pieces or can't move.
